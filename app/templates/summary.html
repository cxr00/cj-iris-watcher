What is this?

This site is a repository for data collected from the Oregon CJ IRIS database at this link.

Data from CJ IRIS is collected daily and compared to data from the day before. Changes, ie additions and removals, are documented and reported here.

This data is imperfect. Still, an effort is made to provide insights into this public data.
