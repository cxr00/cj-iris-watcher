What is this?

This site is a repository for data collected from the Oregon CJ IRIS database at this link.

Data from CJ IRIS is collected daily and compared to data from the day before. Changes, ie additions and removals, are documented and reported here.

So why does it matter?

When a member of the Portland Police Bureau goes undercover, their name and DPSST number is removed from the database.

The goal of this data collection is to spot when those entries are removed, and potentially identify undercover officers.
